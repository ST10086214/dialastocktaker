About Us

Professional stocktaking services you can trust

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Dial a Stocktaker has grown to become one of South Africa's most trusted stocktaking service providers. We started with a simple mission: to help businesses maintain accurate inventory records and improve their bottom line.

Over the years, we've expanded our services and team to meet the growing needs of businesses across various industries, from retail and hospitality to warehousing and manufacturing.

Today, our team of experienced stocktakers serves clients throughout South Africa, combining traditional counting methods with the latest technology to deliver accurate, efficient, and reliable stocktaking services.
