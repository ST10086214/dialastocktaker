Our Services

Comprehensive stocktaking solutions for all your needs

Retail Stocktaking

Our retail stocktaking services are designed to provide accurate inventory counts for businesses of all sizes, from small boutiques to large department stores.

We understand the unique challenges faced by retail businesses and offer flexible scheduling options to minimize disruption to your operations.

What We Offer:

  • Full store counts
  • Sectional counts
  • Pre-sale and post-sale counts
  • Stock reconciliation
  • Shrinkage analysis

Our team uses advanced counting methods and technology to ensure accuracy and efficiency, providing you with reliable data to make informed business decisions.

Warehouse Stocktaking

Our warehouse stocktaking services are comprehensive and adaptable to the specific needs of your facility, whether it's a small storage area or a large distribution center.

We employ efficient methodologies and technology to count and verify inventory in even the most complex warehouse environments.

What We Offer:

  • Full warehouse counts
  • Cycle counting
  • Location verification
  • Barcode scanning and data capture
  • Inventory reconciliation

Our experienced team works efficiently to complete warehouse counts with minimal disruption to your operations, providing accurate results that help optimize your inventory management.

Hospitality Stocktaking

Our hospitality stocktaking services are tailored to the unique needs of restaurants, hotels, bars, and other hospitality businesses.

We understand the fast-paced nature of the hospitality industry and offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your business hours.

What We Offer:

  • Food and beverage inventory
  • Bar stocktaking
  • Kitchen supplies inventory
  • Room amenities counting
  • Cost analysis and reporting

Our specialized hospitality stocktaking services help you control costs, reduce waste, and optimize your ordering processes, ultimately improving your bottom line.

Inventory Reporting

Our comprehensive inventory reporting services provide you with detailed insights into your inventory status, helping you make informed business decisions.

We generate clear, concise reports that highlight key metrics and identify areas for improvement in your inventory management.

What We Offer:

  • Detailed inventory valuation reports
  • Variance analysis
  • Shrinkage reports
  • Stock movement analysis
  • Custom reporting solutions

Our reporting services are customizable to meet your specific needs, providing you with the data and insights needed to optimize your inventory management and improve your bottom line.
